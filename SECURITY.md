# Security Policy

## Supported Scope
This repository contains governance standards and policy documents for Exceeds Group and operating companies.

## Responsible Disclosure
If you identify a security issue in this repository or related standards implementation:
- Do not disclose publicly before coordinated review
- Provide a clear description of the issue and potential impact
- Include reproduction steps and any affected repositories, if known

## Reporting Contact
- `security@exceedsgroup.com`

## Response Process
- Acknowledge receipt within 5 business days
- Assess severity and scope
- Coordinate remediation owners and timelines
- Publish relevant updates to impacted internal stakeholders
- Handle sensitive details on a need-to-know basis until remediation is complete

## Out of Scope
- General product support requests
- Non-security editorial feedback (use standard issue workflow)
